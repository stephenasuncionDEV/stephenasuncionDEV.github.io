Vancouver Police

Call (604) 717-3321. (604) 717-3321 should be used for all non-emergency police situations. Please call 911 to seek immidiate response.
City Services For City Services please contact the city of vancouver by going to this Link. For a quicker response please check our contact page and send a message.
City of Vancouver Website 2 For information on City services, visit the City of Vancouver's website.
City of Vancouver's VanConnect App The city of vancouver actualy has an app called VanConnect app. Where you can search for events, activities, news, and more.
Request a fire report Here. If theres a fire in your community or home please go to the website and click "Find the closes fire hall" and search it up on google then call them. Please call immidiately so that the fire does not reach your neighbors.
Personal Safety If you are scared for your personal safety, or if you feel uncomfortable please immidiately call 911 and we will send a police officer in a matter of seconds. Please state where you are and describe yourself on the phone when you call.
Crime Prevention and Safety If you want to prevent crime in general, please seek self-defence training. Please go to the contact page and tell us what we can do to help you. Our contact page is open for everything. You can ask us anything and we will answer in a matter of seconds.
Identity Theft Vancouver city has a special unit dedicated only for Identity Theft. We make sure no one is using your credit/debit card. We can trace where the culprit is and make them pay in jail.
Financial Crime Vancouver city also has a special unit dedicated only for Financial Crime. If you have experience some kind of Financial Crime. Please contact the Vancouver Police Number for all non-emergency police situations.
